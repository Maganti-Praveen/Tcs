💡 Tips & Advice from Candidates

✅ General Preparation

  • Prepare a strong and structured self introduction (1–2 minutes)
  • Be thorough with every keyword mentioned in your resume
  • Focus on understanding concepts rather than memorizing answers
  • Practice explaining answers clearly and confidently
  • Revise basics from all core subjects before the interview

✅ Be Confident & Honest

Even if you don't know something, be confident and answer in a general way without fear. Be honest — interviewers appreciate honesty over bluffing. If you don't know, handle it smartly instead of guessing blindly.

✅ Project Preparation

  • Prepare end-to-end explanation of your project
  • Be ready for deep technical cross-questions from your project
  • Know why you chose specific technologies
  • Prepare real-time use cases and problem statements
  • Be ready to explain code, architecture, and workflow
  • Mention: problem statement → approach → tools used → outcome

✅ Programming & DSA

  • Practice basic coding problems daily
  • Focus on arrays, strings, recursion, and patterns
  • Learn optimization techniques (time and space complexity)
  • Be able to explain your code step by step
  • Revise OOP concepts with real-time examples

✅ SQL & DBMS

  • Practice writing queries without syntax errors
  • Focus on joins, GROUP BY, aggregate functions
  • Learn queries like max salary, second max, top N records
  • Understand differences: TRUNCATE vs DELETE vs DROP
  • Practice real-time database scenarios

✅ AI/ML & Advanced Technologies

  • Prepare basic definitions and differences clearly
  • Understand concepts like overfitting, bias-variance tradeoff
  • Learn use cases of ML models
  • Be ready to explain models used in your project
  • Have basic knowledge of trending topics (LLM, RAG, Generative AI)

✅ Cloud Technologies

  • Learn basics of cloud computing and service models (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS)
  • Understand AWS core services (EC2, S3, IAM)
  • Be ready to justify if you haven't done certifications
  • Know real-time use cases of cloud services

✅ Web Development

  • Revise HTML, CSS, and basic JavaScript
  • Understand frontend vs backend clearly
  • Learn API integration basics
  • Be ready to explain your full stack project flow

✅ Know Complexity

After writing code, be ready to explain its time complexity and space complexity.

✅ OSI Model

Prepare the OSI Model layers especially if you have a networking / cybersecurity background.

✅ Real-World Examples

For OOP concepts and ML algorithms, giving real-world examples adds great value to your answer.

✅ Behavioral & HR Round

  • Prepare answers for common HR questions
  • Be honest and natural in your responses
  • Maintain a positive attitude even in tricky questions
  • Show flexibility for relocation and shifts
  • Handle situational questions with logical thinking

✅ For Experienced Candidates

  • Be clear about your previous work and responsibilities
  • Prepare system design basics (APIs, databases, scaling)
  • Be ready for scenario-based and real-world questions
  • Justify your switch clearly

✅ Duration

TR+MR round is typically 20–35 minutes. HR round is typically 15–25 minutes. Be concise and clear.

✅ Final Tips

  • Stay calm and confident during the interview
  • Maintain good communication and eye contact
  • Listen carefully before answering
  • Interviews can vary panel to panel — be adaptable
  • If you don't know an answer, handle it smartly instead of guessing blindly
